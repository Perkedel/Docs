# Deltarune New Paradigm Repeat without delete account / save

the Deltarune: New Paradigm gameplay is based on Zenless Zone Zero. Basically, the Main Story system is converted into segmented by Quest.

The Chapter Quests are inserted into each respective VHS Tape like the original. When you start the account, you'll automatically plays the Tape seamlessly.

Best thing of this New Paradigm is that it lets you do mistake. Unlike original, New Paradigm removes the requirement to delete your whole account just to remedy a teeny tiny mistake on that chapter. All you have to do, is to start the VHS Tape again, after you finished a chapter, i.e. not starting a chapter Tape.  
Mind you, if e.g. your action affects next chapters and you're not interested of that outcome will be, you must redo that numbers of previous chapters, and then another, before you play the current one. But hey, you don't have to delete your account or *alt email* just to remedy those all!

Now, on some chapters, the transition between requires you to continue in order to maintain its immersiveness. You can now pause the Main Story and wander off a while, and for during this chapter, at certain state, it can add bonus progress to whichever chapter it goes. Well up to you, coz Duet Night Abyss broke one anyway. Obviously it's recommended to keep going when the game recommends you so.  
Extra NPC Activities are now switchable at random chance when you are not Tracking Main Story (Quest Menu, Untrack the Red Mission; or Have all Main Stories finished). Simply use Time Change feature, it'll shuffle between various NPC activities, for both Light World & Dark World.

Speaking of ZZZ, Deltarune combat system is still Honkai: Star Rail, i.e. Turn based. Don't worry, we have also improved the auto system.  
In this Auto system, you can choose the mode, whether to go Pacifist (default), Genocide, or random whatever.  
Beware, on DETERMINATION ♥️🎮 defense, you still need to add few manual intervention, the Auto system does move also your character mark, but it succs at dodging.
