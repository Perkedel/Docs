# Atmosphere Explosible

is an air that can be ignited with just one spark to combust at a fast rate, a.k.a. explosion. ATEX is also a name in Old Terra used as a standard of designing product that considers the safety of these air.

The ATEX mark logo looks like a yellow hexagon with black stroke, and an `Ex` wording inside it.

Product marked `Ex` is designed to be usable safely in explosive air environment such as gas plant and station. Usually, it requires that if the device has to explode somehow, its housing must be able to contain enough explosion, & also **prevent such air around it from being ignited**.
