### Convert Wii Redump compressed Format

your [Dolphin emulator](https://dolphin-emu.org/) can convert `.rvz` files back to ISO. `.rvz` is unconventional compressed format that Myrient only offers.

- Plop in the rvz file to your library folder you added to Dolphin already.
- The game should appear in the dashboard. Right click & Convert. Follow instruction!
