# The Tale of King with Gradual Syndrome

## Prologue

Once upon a time lived a king ruling the kingdom. He is very humble and often provides subsidy to his people in need. He also provided education on how to have the same capability as him, so when the senior is unavailable, they can empower themselves. He also taught them to also depend on another, because everyone is different, and nobody cannot do things alone, inevitably.

The mission is never done. Everyday, a new wish comes, and the king never feels satisfied. King kept having more wishes and so far all has been fullfilled everytime. Right from his people, his families, and himself.

## Problem

Alas, suddenly, one day, the king fell ill. Immediately, he invited not only his personal medic but also all doctors which each has one representative. Such news creates gloom on all ends, the king shall not fell like this, he never did something wrong during his life, and he always do fitness at times. The doctors analyzed the king, wrangled, discussed, and built the conclusions. These representatives are the brightest Doctorates every hospital can have.  
Gravely enough, despite of all the effort, it was concluded that the king has a Genetic Syndrome. Similar case to Stephen Hawking. The king told all doctors to keep it a secret for now, and had to propaganda that doctors are working on it and he's stable. Clearly he's not. This syndrome is worse, his lifespan cut short.

Devastation. Feel of the doom. The end of the era is nigh. But the king is genius. While he still got the genius left, he does something before he lost it all

- Backup. One of the doctor representing a hospital has a best friend who's a scientist that made a device that can scan your brain and also wrangle every wish you had in it. Unfortunately the memory is not so big to hold all of his evergrowing wishes. The disk for it that's compatible is currently also few. So he only put the most magnum opus all as he can there.
- Initiative. He added a new quest for citizens to join in. An initiative that's basically a game company that purposed to realize said wishes. These are all the wishes.
- Understanding. The king also updated the scope of independence & dependency to not only around the kingdom but also to outside, while also being careful of what is good and what is bad.

And all other he done as all as he can.

## Climaxing

It was foretold such here. Day by day, the king's condition kept getting worse. From the slurred, loss of speech, loss of motors, and loss of anything that's important, till the critical where he lost his genius. But with the backup, initiative, humbleness, understanding, etc., Some still not lost. Everyone at the initiative has undefined deadline before the king's death. Other citizens are excited to see the release of the product based on the king's wishes. The king does everything he can to monitor everything that he established, here and out, and to interact whatever he could so that it's perfect as perfect for his eyes and the people.

Fortunately, there's some times left. At last, the Initiative has finished the version, and it's ready to release. Because it was for king's wishes, the king was set to be the first that tried the product. It's a *stable* version of the product btw, one that is literally ready to use to the public. He done all the part of the product in 3 days. So far so good. Some bugfixes were made, and other workers retested it. The king said that it's ready to release. And so, the countdown begins.

The countdown is going to reach zero, but suddenly the king lost his conciousness! Oh no! The king must be awake when the customers use it! what do we do?! The doctors are panicking. Heart stable, but the brain shows anomalies that basically means unconcious. Then the doctors invited all hospitals in the kingdom's contact list, all of the doctors who are not in duty in this land. They have to CPR but the brain! All of them debugging. Trying to extract everything they could. The countdown is 30 seconds left, if the product released when he's not awake, we're done!

Alas, the countdown passes over. And the king still not waking up. He's stuck on his own limbo! But hey, do you guys remember of one of the pillar says? Understanding! The king needs us! So, in the feedback, let's add a field to have then send good wishes to him! And so, everybody filled the survey and got rewarded as usual, with this time also includes real cash. It was a tax return for being loyal to the king after all these years. Then, the doctors setup a globe OLED screen that displays all the reactionary wishes about the product. If there's nothing work, at least we can use spiritual and mental way in order to spark recovery on our king! And so, the screen began to display the comments, and doctors do one more attempt. 

Zap!, suddenly the king's finally awake. Awake now, in a sea of positive feedbacks

> Get well soon!

> Wow this gameplay unique!

> A game where you can steth women heartbeats?! Bring all my friends in!

> There's no other companies dare to make goon as daring as this one! Game industries, this is your lesson!

and it goes for the next 1 trillion of them.

The king smiles. At last, his final wishes that could be backed up have been fullfilled. His pondering becomes soft, like he's ascending to heaven, for so many not only looking for it, but also full with sympathy & empathy. All his behaviour that he shared, and the lesson he shared to all citizens, the humbleness, everything. Are worth the effort for generations to come.

## Inevitably

And so, it went quiet afterward. Worryingly, so quiet. One of the doctor had a terrible feeling, so she immediately lift up the globe screen. Yep, as been felt. He was, ... ***home***.  
Before this, all involved doctors were shared each copy of his will. That if he ultimately has to Go Home, the Initiative will be kept running under the leadership of his first child, the prince. And the education will be kept running under the leader of his second child, the princess. Until they grow to the maturity respectively, the butlers will hold those administration while also mentoring those children. And when this ultimately has to happen, the secrets are no longer, and all shall not be hidden no more. Truly inevitable sorrow, but at least, his magnum opus had came to fruition at least well, first.

The lives at the kingdom continues. The product kept being improved over and over, until a few decades later when it has to EOS and the product turned into Archive. But thanks to the inspirative story of the king, such Rabbithole and the derivatives kept being discussed for even more years to come, and to this day centuries later, it refused to die. Even when the product itself exhausted, part of the Rabbithole are many times implemented on many other products, from medical, down to a whole new entertainment beyond ever been imagined.
