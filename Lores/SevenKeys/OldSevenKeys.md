## Seven Keys? What meaning was that?

### What is Seven Keys?
I was thinking of a subreddit name randomly, then this pops up on my mind.

Since that happened, I decided to create one, before I forgot again.

So I am now thinking what Seven Keys do to something.

It could be something related. A story in Perkedel Cinematic Universe, so that would be a video game film, a DVD for Hexagon Engine. Idk.

For now its here, for stuff about anything that requires 7 key components in order to accomplish something.

### Oh, so what can I post here?
Everything. Any story, experience, whatever that involves:

- 7 required things
- 7 keys (literal, or not)
- 7 piece of puzzle
- in multiplication of 7. Series of 7 multiplications.
- Prime Number
- etc.

Go ahead, show your abstracts of 7 Keys.
