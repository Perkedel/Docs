# Burning House

A building shaped Object Anomaly. Burning House is a wooden house that's always burning. But its flame never burn anyone or any object by default.

Burning House can make thugs like Garuda goons fried, fire works as usual, but at the same time the innocents and other items remain unharmed.

Burning House hates dry ice, it suffocates it. If you engulf enough dry ice to it, it extinguish and the whole house nullifies. But any other extinguisher won't hurt the house.
