# Deepseek (Oddity)

Deepseek is a hologram oddity that lets you spawn images of your desire and anwering other questions. It has keyboard that lets you type in your inquiry, and Deepseek will answer the best it could.

## Public Facility

Deepseek is very often used by town managements and tenants across Hetherau. They not only use it for insights but also detailings. Deepseek never ask anything in exchange but an appreciation. It just spits an image poster with size according to target surface.

> You can't just use AI like that. You're a bank! A fucking capable company! You supposed to be able to commission someone no big deal! The fuck, you cut corners like nothing?

Because using Exchange Free oddity is considered cheating, people on Hetherau have been assembling activism in revolt against Deepseek kind. Anyone that dared to make business with Deepseek will be labeled various derogatory remarks, including `clanker lover`.
