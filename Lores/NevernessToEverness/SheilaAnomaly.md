# Sheila

Sheila Anomaly is an anomaly that generates shotgun shell, hence the name, Sheila. 

It feeds on plastics and a little bit of metal scraps.

Sheila shapes like end cap of a shotgun, particularly M1014 model. You just replace the gun's endcap with this Anomaly thing. And you can fire virtually infinite ammo.

Be careful, if Sheila is starving and you failed to dip Sheila into scraps of plastic and metal in 12 days, it will nullify itself, and you no longer have infinite ammo.

Sheila can be harvested from Shelltree, an anomaly shaped like tree that bears fruits which is Sheila.
