# Esper not working in Perkedel

Esper and Anomaly powers malfunctions in Perkedel without Cardiochip system worn and enabled on them.

Anomalies, including Oddities, also lose their power there. Some Anomalies immediately nullifies once entered Perkedel (and respawn accordingly as usual if they can).

## Vice versa

Return to original NTE world to restore working condition of Esper and Anomaly powers.

And so, other powers from other universe may automatically work here. e.g., Originium Arts works here without requiring Cardiochips.
