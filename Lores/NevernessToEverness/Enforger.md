# Enforger

an Object Anomaly. It's a photocopier machine that can print ultra realistic fake Fons.

Financially, this is formidable anomaly to be let live, because it always generate Fons for nothing out of thin air. Its fake Fons printed even validable, and if just 1 entered circulation, that could crash the whole economy unbeknownstly.

Nullifying this photocopier is easy. Just throw it to a jail cell, it'll vapourize itself.
