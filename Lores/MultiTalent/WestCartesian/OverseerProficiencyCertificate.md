The Overseer Proficiency Certificate is a license exam that assess your worth of bearing extra Overseer features. Every Overseer will start with whatever DNB provides. But there are other features which only those who are worthy of bearing such, because e.g., those feature are very sensitive and if abused, can cause fatal disaster. That is why every sometimes, DNB always provide a voluntary exam that grants you this certificate to unlock those extra features.  

Mind you, doing this exam may not be easy as being memorized of the answer.
