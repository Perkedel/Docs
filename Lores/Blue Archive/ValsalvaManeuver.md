# Valsalva Maneuver experiment

## Ingredients

- Made up heart organ
- Thorax box. Or a pressure chamber, whatver to represent chest cavity. Recommended the clear ones.

## Procedure

- Setup heart in thorax box.
  - Make the heart organ controlled using chemical wires, like naturally is.
  - Pressure to `default`
  - Breathing `ON`
  - Air Valves `auto`, opened & default operation
- turn on heart
- invoke `Adrenaline`s until heart reach at least 200 BPM
- Observe
- breathe out & close all Air Valves
- take a deep breath while Air Valves still closed.
- Observe. This is Negative Valsalva. Heart should reset back to default rate. If skipping, it's normal.
- (Optional) Repeat the process but inverse, Positive Valsalva!
  - breathe in to increase pressure
  - close all Air Valves
  - breathe out while Air Valves still closed.
