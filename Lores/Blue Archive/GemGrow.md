# Gem Grow

https://youtube.com/short/Yy4kuG_MP6w

Chikrovski Experiment

## Ingredients

- a small Copper crystal. That blue crystal. at least 2 cm^3
- bath of copper sulphate. Also blue.
- Dowel & sticky clay at the end
- Holding arm stand
- (optional) gem cutting machine
- Clearcoat paint spray

## Step

- stick the copper crystal onto the dowel & clay.
- attach the stuck dowel to arm stand
- make that rig dip the crystal onto the bath
- wait a while
- the crystal grows! Stop at anytime your desired size
- You can also do the similar step like above making silicone crystal by slowly growing the crystal bottom by bottom. You need a machine that can pull dowel slowly as the crystal grows in the bottom alike of that computer chip crystal growing process. You will get rod crystal like above video.
- Once done, you may also trim and shape the crystal with gem cutting machine to further optimize the shape.
- If you are satisfied, you can spray clearcoat to bake and lock the shape.
