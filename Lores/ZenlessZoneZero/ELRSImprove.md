# Improve the ExpressLRS

## Begin

ExpressLRS is an open source wireless protocol designed for reliable industry controls. ELRS is very common on RCs. It can reach very far, and industries can make it cheap and very reliable.

But of course, ELRS can only do so much without the optimizations made for today's situation, **Hollow**. How should ELRS compensate the disturbance of Hollow?

The ELRS technology is very ancient, but has been used for over 80 years, to this day in Perkedel Cinematic Universe. Today, the RadioComms & Phaethon are going to research how to improve the ELRS so it stands against Hollow's disturbance.

Taking place after the Fresh Install. Event not-limited time

Eich found that the Hollow that appeared in Lancaster's world is the exact Hollow as in the ZZZ universe. You will do a gameplay fighting serveral enemies in a selected Hollow place. In the story you'll also do another mission at the same time, it is to put some kind of markers inside certain places in Hollow, so you'll just go to destined spot, & interact it.
