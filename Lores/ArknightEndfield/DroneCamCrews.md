# Drone Camera Crews

Inside a Flying Camera Needles, there are

- Pilot. Move the drone
- Manager. Overview the operation
- Cameraman. Operate the camera.

They listen to the Command, when to move to the next spot and aim transform the Needle drone and the camera to designated transform in each spot.

## example

Command: O16 (Oscar One six), prepare to move to the next spot (on your Dashboard screen)

Pilot: Moving, arrive, steady.

Manager: camera (to be selected) 5 sec!

Camera: Aim, Spot!

Managaer: (camera) Select (-ed)!
