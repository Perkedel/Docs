# Rebuild Shenzen??

It's been 300 years passed since on our Terra. Endministrator & friend visited China & restored stuffs there. Shenzen like other city is a tech city. After the *Threat* has ransacked the city, Endmin & friend rebuilt the city to make it work again. When they arrived, everyone surprised, how could the *Threat* be there. This isn't a human or LB issues, there were *Threats*.

Shenzen contains lots of Predefined Automation Lines. Develop this map to unlock more lines. Here in Shenzen, the lines manufactures cool gadgets, some of which can be gifted to your Operators.
