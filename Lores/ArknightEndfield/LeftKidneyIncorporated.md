# Left Kidney Incorporated

Left Kidney Inc. is a company focused on making filters, especially food grade surgical ones. A spin-off of Arenod Sediron Heart Organ manufacturing house, Left Kidney produces kidneys and various dialysis parts and systems for various industry types.

Accidentally, Left Kidney destroys the kidney as diamond valuings as they managed to mass produce standard kidneys for humans. Like Duron's stock heart organs, their kidneys are high quality and always compatible, thanks to the same technique used, which is bespoke STEM cell commanding technique.

Also, like before, Left Kidney has improved kidneys for various needs. E.g., to filter Originium off to piss, to significantly reduce chance of Oripathy. Along with other premium kidneys. But basically, standard kidney should be enough, and like Duron's company told, **no need to replace still working from-born kidneys**.

Left Kidney also makes other kinds of filtration systems that's inspired from how Kidneys works, osmosisly. They have water filtration systems used to process waste before it thrown to gutters, with clients like [Embarrasser River](/Lores/ArknightEndfield/EmbarrasingRiver.md), Salt brine filter, etc etc.

Left Kidney is a member of Pacto FFYO (FFYO's philantrophy organization) to make sure product price stays affordable, and allows subsidy for the financial troubled regions.
