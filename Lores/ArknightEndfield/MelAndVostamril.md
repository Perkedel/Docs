# Mel Starasch and Vostamril Kashekov

Sequel of [this](/Lores/Uncategorized/MelTheEgyptianCat.md).

Now these are couple.

They were one of the Parim's Live Eternal experiment participant. Therefore they not only live to be ZZZ agent, but now also Endfield Operators you can play.

Mel still works as the mission command like she did in [Lah-Mboh](https://github.com/Perkedel/Lah-Mboh). She is Cryo Striker 6. She is a Caster. She likes to stab an ice, so Combo Skill activates when an Operator hit Finisher Final Strike. Activate this to stab the ice. Best combined with Operator with Combo Skill thay activates after Mel stabbed an ice or an elemental to complete an Infliction.  
Activate Ultimate for her second stance 30s. her Basic Attack works like Laevatain Ultimate 2nd stance, giving huge DMG. 3 clicks of Skill arms `Pen Explosion` like Laevatain. Activate Skill again for this 3rd time to cast duar ice to target and around it for massive DMG comparable to Ye Shunguang. This will force *Solidification**. Then break the frozen enemies there (with Physical skill) to add extra `Es Mambo` Post-Infliction `Shatter` DMG.

And Vostamril is a Physical Support 5. Combo Skill activates when an Operator HP (including himself) goes below 50%. Use Combo Skill `Pushing Through` to heal that Operator. Base cooldown 15s. There is an upgrade that adds 2nd Combo Skill `Vampirism`, activate when an Operator cast Finisher. Activating this skill absorbs 15% HP immediately off the target and share it flat across all Operators. Or, if an Operator HP is 15% below and is lowest, it will exclusively be given to that or himself (if he's the one below 15% left and lowest).

The CEO was arrested and prosecuted. But to fullfill CEO's wish of lost doohickey recovery, DNB prepared another armies, armed with full blown weapons as a defense and logistic retrieval sets to recover all of the lost tech.

It took 20 years to finally recover the last tech ever. No casualties thankfully, everyone is a fighter and knows their Arts.
