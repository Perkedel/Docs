Encryptium symbol is 🛑 octagon. Like related to Originium the 🔷 rhombus.

Their eyes have octagon symbols there on pupil, but default is invisible. When they doing strong Encryptium Arts, those octagon reveals, just like rhombus on Originium based agent.
