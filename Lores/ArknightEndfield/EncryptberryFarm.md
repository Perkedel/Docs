# Encryptberry farm

The Encryptberry fruityard is very wide, spans hundreds of hectares

They harvest using alien-like beaming. So, the drones (using Stork Cargo Vore) open their belly and absorb the ripe fruits beamingly. It has very smart AI that only picks ripe Encryptberries.  
Once full, they'll close their belly, and fly to the Collection Funnel.

In the Funnel, the Drones lands, drop the berries, and pipe those onto these carts. The carts then brought to another house next line for further processings, such as selection, cleansing, and packaging.
