## Make your own Chubby Lung RC!

You will need

- RC model of Ranger car / "Jeep" with roof open
- Chubby Lung figurine
- Remote

Make it!

1. Prepare both Model & Remote. I recommend to use ExpressLRS based drive controller, so you can use any common ELRS remote.
2. Attach Chubby Lung in the driver seat, by the steering wheel of the model
3. Enjoy
