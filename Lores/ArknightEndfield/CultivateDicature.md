## Food

You can use Dicature to craft HQ Nutritional foods, such as Skewer BBQ (Sate). The meat uses Agagrit (Beef) or Fowlbeast (Chicken).

Eating Sate of either kind restores 12% HP per sec for 30 seconds, triggered when HP under 75%. Max 3 use. But other operator give you one is unlimited.

## AIC

You can loop farm Dicature. Set the Planter to **Water mode**.

Hence, for Farm plot version needs to be watered once you planted. It also has mutation chance when harvested, increasable when fertilized.

## Mutation

Dicature has mutation variant called Puffy Dicature. Has consistency as soft as Higashi Sticky Beans. Basically soy. Indeed used to make that snack, Tempe, and various other fermented Dicature snacks.

You can use this to craft buff medicine, by powdering it first and pack them in a container, respectively in quality. In the story it adds extra pecal nutritions from inside. But in game, it also adds DEF & ATK, and increases Crit chances, all these for 5 minutes.

If you used Puffed Dicature to make Sate, the Skewer magically turns to **Gilded Maranggi BBQ**. It's basically Sate Maranggi with special taste. Has 3 times the the buff. I recommend using Agagrit version, that's the most popular one.
