# Gravity Plush Indicator.

Astronauts brings plushie to their ship as a tradition. But, it's also scientific. The doll acts as a orientative indicator regarding physical status. E.g., if you have entered orbit onto a planet, the doll will move and gradually fell down more closer you to its ground.  
Secondly, plush doll is usually soft, very important. When it relatively moves and bump onto stuffs, it won't damage equipments.
