# Drone "Bestie"

Emilia's personal Needles quadcopter.

she named this private not-jet `Bestie`. When she was 6, her dad, Jeff, taught her alot about drone ridings. Jeff when he was an Interpol ride lots of helicopters delivering soldiers here and there. When Dasandim introduced quads, Jeff was the first to be assigned. And now, this enthusiasm inherited to his kids, including Emilia.

This quad was customized just for Emilia. The seat was molded bespoke like for motorsport racers, allowing her to be comfortable, especially if she had to fly long. The control mimics RC FPV, which is Mode 2, where there is a throttle on the left, and grab joystick on the right. It also has smart ECU making it intuitive to control, just like Acro in the RC. In fact, Emilia's case becomes the default control for the rest of the Needles drones.

It also has technomagics, allowing this not-jet flown Acro, sly, agile, and fast, complete with flips, rolls, and all that crazy stunts, like 1 to 1 with RC. So buckle up, because that's Emilia's favourite activity when riding Bestie. I suggest, if you are not into Roller Coaster or Fighter Jet, to avoid riding with Emilia at all cost. She will always stunts like Rossi's extra pose when walking and running.

Don't worry too much. We have taught Emilia to not drop land like all RC pilots do, because this is a huge drone. You could break the ground, e.g.
