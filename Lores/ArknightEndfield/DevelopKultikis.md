# Develop Kultikis

sequel of [ArcPerc](/Lores/Uncategorized/ArcPercDicatureX0p1r4t3.md), see also [Dicature](CultivateDicature.md).

## What?

Kultikis Independent City. Home to many factories. This is the city where many of your stuffs gets manufactured. They got plots for manufacturings, warehouse, and other kinds of B2B facilities.

Kultikis is also the originator of Dicature beans. This fruitbean known for special cosmetic designed for pectorals. To this day is the largest farm and processors, and have 25k+ distribution partners & uncountable splintering farms outside Kultikis. The seed can also be crushed to make special BBQ sauce, used to craft Gilded Maranggi and other types of Skewer BBQ. Ferment the seed, you can craft Tempe, Higashi-style nougats, and more.

## AIC

You'll have side quest to develop Kultikis. Once you accomplished set of those quest, you'll unlock Sub-PAC here. The area from start is very wide compared to many other regions Sub-PAC.

## Trading

Trade the Dicature Cream. 3 Quality levels from `C`, `B`, then `A` the highest.

Making Dicature Cream has been difficult as of recent. Currently, their cream could only puff up pecs about 12% than Control. You can use the AIC to grind, refine, hydrate and pack the cream to be sold back to them.

They also accept Raw Dicature and various basic items too

## Dicature Cream

Gives you 12% HP a sec for 30 seconds. Max 3 use, but other Operators give you one is unlimited

the mutated variant, **Puffed Dicature**, can be used to craft Dicature Ointment. Immediately fullfill your HP and fullfil Skill tokens.

## BBQ

Craft Skewer BBQ! You can use Aggeloifowl meat or Agagrit meat as the base, and Dicature as the BBQ sauce. Gives you 1000 HP immediately, and buff effect you ATK Boost + Increased CRIT Chance for 5 minutes.

If you used its mutated variant, **Puffed Dicature**, the Skewer magically turns into **Gilded Maranggi**. It's a Kultikis Signature dish, typically uses Agagrit. buff effect multiplies 3 times from former.

## Pectoral Expansions

Kultikis people used Dicature therapy to expand breasts, since its effect discovery.

Additionally, Kultikis also conceived a Dicature laser beam to maximize the result. Now available as a cosmestic clinic service one can buy for. Notable example being [Heather Lyton](/Lores/Uncategorized/ArcPercDicatureX0p1r4t3.md), cousin of Admiral Zumi.
