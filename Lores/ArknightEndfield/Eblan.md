# 👽 Eblan

## What is that?

Eblan is that 👽 mainstream overused alien being. Same that big eyes, long inverted egg shaped head. Shape that's related to [Howard the Alien](https://odysee.com/@X0P1R4T3-33NSVREENMNT-0FFICIAL:f/howard-the-alien-fbx:3).

Funny story, before this happen, such alien that looks like that never exist. It was a Hollywoodian depiction of what an extraterrestrial being could be. the *👽* shape was one of them.  
The how this now exist is quite baffling. Once upon a time, circa 2150, there's group of former Terrans who chose to leave Terra and live on Mars. They now become Scientist years later. One day, they did an experiment inspired from Arenod Sediron. They are not satisfied with how it work, coz without the Arenod Sediron air, it's unreliable. So, they chose traditional way. And so, they made mutation avatar. Consequences is that they can't turn back. No worries, they are also no longer interested with human looks anymore. And heck, they got cool buffs like Airless breathing!. So that's where we are now.

Now, in Frutiger Aero Capsule (Sophia Doohickey's *Dijiang* but Perkedel shaped), you'll meet these Eblans in one of the main stories.  
Ever since the arrival of Endfield technologies for Earth, Eblans goes extremely envious. They dislike the idea of serving back the oh-so-broken Old Terra, so they wanted bigger shares than it. Thankfully, Endmin reconciled them, and offered Eblan the same fair amount of technologies for their houses too as well.
