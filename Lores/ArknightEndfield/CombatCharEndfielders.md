## Team Idea

### Combo Infliction-Reaction

Combo Infliction means doing an Arts Infliction, and then do the second one which cause **Arts Reaction** effect. **Arts Reaction** effect will be added to the enemy, and it gave overtime Arts DMG.

The formula goes like: `any (a / b / c) + d`.

- either Inflict `a`, `b`, `c` element, other than its target element you'd want the Burst to be.
- then inflict `d` element after that to get **Arts Reaction**. There are:
  - Cryo = Solidification
  - Heat = Combustion
  - Electric = Electrification
  - Nature = Corrosion

### Physical Status

Physical has status variants on its own:

- Lift
- Knock Down
- Crush
- Breach

(for **New Paradigm** only, where below Reactions added) Unlike Combo Reaction, causing Reaction with a valid according Physical Status will not consume that token. You can now stack em up more before the Physical Status time runs out.

Remember, before you can cause Physical Status, most often you need to have atleast 1 **Vulnerable** beforehand. So do the first skill (any kind) is to add a **Vulnerable** then second time (Physical) to activate the actual Physical Status. Some Operators have skills (usually in their Ultimate) that can immediately cause Physical Status without needing **Vulnerability**.
