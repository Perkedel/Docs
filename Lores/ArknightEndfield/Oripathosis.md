# Oripathosis

A similar effect of Miasma corruption in Zenless Zone Zero a century ago.

If you are in Oripathosis zone, you'll see a special corruption meter. This meter will accumulates in second, and when it's full, the Oripathosis activates.  
Oripathosis meter will also increase if you are hit by an Oripathy inducing enemy.

- Originium based power (Originium, Encryptium, Oripathy, Encryptopathy, Reconvener) triggers involutarily and very violent, e.g.
  - Endmin. Spawn Originium stabs from ground 10 metres around
  - Tangtang. Water balloon like explosion pushing enemies up to 50 meters away and inflict base 2500 DMG
  - Emilia. Radio Interference, electrocute enemy causing them lose mobility in place for 30 seconds and inflict 500 DMG per second during that window.
- Lose 25% of HP & 75% of DEF. an upgrade can decrease this consequence

Oripathosis zone consists of

- Nicotine smoke

To make Oripathosis works, you are required to have the (scene air be filled with Originium / Encryptium || Agent is Originium / Oripathy / Encryptium / Encryptopathy / Reconvener), && above zone.
