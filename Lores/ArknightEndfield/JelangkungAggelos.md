# Jelangkung

a boss Aggelos based on horror item Jelangkung.

This Aggelos has combination of steel and typical rocks material.

She has the same metal ball cut open head like Ethereal variants back in ZZZ days. This time it's hollow, and instead a usual halo on top of the head.  
also has the same metal stick pole body with rocks covering them.

she also floats like Ethereal variant.

Despite the hollow head, it now serves as skill. If you fail to parry red circle, she will cast Ultimate which fires lethal laser from her coconut head spin 360°.

Her cloths are made of Blight jelly that shields her body, and hoods her head. Unfortunately, you cannot damage it even with Enhanced Xiranite spray.
