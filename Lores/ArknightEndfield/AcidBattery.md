# Acid Battery

An automotive battery with core liquid based on Percipitative Acid. Nodes are made of Ferium & Cuprium. It's a simple battery that says Acids can be electrical too when fetched right way.

When at some point you are unable to obtain the most efficient material for your power units, you can always reach for the fossil oil of those Aggeloi, make em up & call it a day, atleast for now until you out of survival mode.
