The conveyor can now be place however you want like cable. Each machine can also be placed above shims too! You can even stack different machines! With this you can crumble your manufacturing systems, leaving more space for more systems, waaw!!

You also get more Logistics system like Vertical lifts & pump to move materials in an extreme angles!

## How to Unlock

Do the Encrypten's Chapter of Main Story. You can skip and start right away, but I recommended you to finish all original Endfield stories down to the EOS ones.

During the middle of story, you'll encounter AIC segment about this. Once you've finished the sales pitch, you'll get the first Encrypten AIC Core. Now you can open Encrypten AIC Chart I with it.
