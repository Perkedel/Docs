# Endmin's Fartium

Basically a gas composed of Originium. Of course, it's the Embarrassers labeled it.

They also labeled Encryptium's version as Emilia's Fartium.

Both of these gas kinds are basically waste products of other manufacturings, which such burried garbages turned into gas in a matter of weeks. The Doohickers and partners have been using it e.g. as a part of a Recyclement program.

You can also replicate this phenomenon with the AIC module of **Fermentator**, where you take place of waste fluids and solids into it. It'll output respective fart gas at the fastest down to 1 second.
