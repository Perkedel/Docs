# Eye candy game

Jackbox earwax but video

each player submit max 3 videos from available Library in a server of the session.

a stage has randomly generated prompt. design your choices based on the prompt.

then players vote all other player choice (except own) of video choices. Highest vote scores. You can vote what's the most funny, cursed, or whatever you'd like, combination of choices.

highest vote when all defined number of stages finished, wins.
