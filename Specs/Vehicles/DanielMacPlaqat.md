# Daniel Mac Plaqat

A special sticker pasted at each front door near the rearview mirrors. It's inspired from [Daniel Mac](https://youtube.com/@itsdanielmac)'s action of interviewing random strangers on street about themselves and what they do, while they're stopping by in their car.

The plaqat sticker contains spec details of

- Model Make Price. The car brand, model, and revision year. As well as how much this car generally cost.
- Callsign. Owner username & optionally other chat network or DNB Compat callsign.
- Occupation. What do you do
- Steer mark. Mark appears if the interviewer interviewed from wrong side. E.g. for DNB, Indonesia, Japan, UK, has `Wrong side` mark on the port side so to tell them to move to another side which is starboard.
