# Hand Pedals

Hand pedals are the controls at the floor brought to the steering wheel.

Because not all region would've had easily the surgery needed to restore functioning legs. As well as if there has been, the being themeselves had no "foot" in the beginning. How'd they suppose to hit the pedal then?

Remember using gamepad to drive the car in a game? Remember you twist the starboard handle to gas in a motorcycle? We now brought it here! Now your steering wheel has a mini pedals to actually (R / twist R) accelerate & (L / pulltab) brake. For Racetrack, there's a new mini pedal / button at the shifter for clutch. All these pedals / buttons work just like your typical gamepad, is analog and with feedbacks! Just like regular foot pedals!

Now, not only paraplegic can drive, even beings who has no concept of foot can drive too, yay!!!!

and best of all, **ALL MODELS, Racetrack & Van Elektronische have them!!!** Pick your model and trim, & start driving!

## Why

- Both hands stays on wheel! Just like both hand shall be on the handle in motorcycle. It's should've been nothing, but solutions that existed are so [rudimentary and kludgy](https://youtu.be/M-zq0bZG3uQ)! Our solution that you should've adapt from us ensure it works just like riding motorcycle, but it's wheel. In fact scooters put it all on steering handles! Gas, and both side of brake pulltabs, all in the handlebar!
- Open Source Patent. Not only because of Volvo, we believe thickly of Open Source for the Good in general. Zero royalty free, Mandatory in DNB and had to be elsewhere.
