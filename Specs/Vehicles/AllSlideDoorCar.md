# All Slide Doors Car

The first car that all doors are sliding, ala taxi airplanes.

Finally, you can open door in a tight spot and a bunch of improper parkers!

The middle doors slide as usual. The front doors, slide forward! Just pull OPEN handle, and push forward like opening airplane hatch door! Even the back trunk has break halves gate technology, so you can open up even if you parked up tight.

All doors electric with manual override always available when needed! With metal skeleton on the handles and fire retardant materials.

Available as EV in Van Elektronische & ICE or Tandem in Racetrack. Pick yours!
