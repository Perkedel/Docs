# Fire Extinguisher Belt

The belt that extinguish fire if exposed to flame.

When that part touches a material at a certain temperature, that part will detonate and explode a snow that extinguish the hot thing.

You can e.g. wrap it around electricals. Say there's a short circuit and the thermal runs away. The belt will instantly extinguish the runaway before it becomes forest fire.

All Van Elektronische parent company cars is among the many items in DNB that ship this built-in. You can find these in Batteries (or fuel tank for RaceTrack), Motors (or Engine for RaceTrack), Electrical system (from Accu, Cables, and Computers), and other thermal sensitive components around.
