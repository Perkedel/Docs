# Marathon Funny

Marathon with doohickeys on the way

## Athletes

they wear special suit with tracking chips. Inspired from mocap suit, the chips proximitily interacts with the doohickeys and gives them bonus points.

## example doohickeys

- Skip Jumps. Jump pad to overhead bridge to overtake other athletes all above way.
- Stone jump. Just jump with only according number of legs.
- Treadmill. Sonic-like booster jumpstarter. interact and get speed push
