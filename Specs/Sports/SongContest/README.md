# Mosfier Song Contest

Inspired from Eurovision Song Contest, MSC is a sport where one artist represent a country or an independent city in coverage of DNB Union compete against each other who shall take the prize, which is a WIN Trophy.

the MSC was originally because to rip off ESC, but extended to a submission form for high quality royalty free & open source songs. Every songs submitted belongs to DNB's record label, Mosfier Records and copyrighted fully under Perkedel which is CC4.0-BY-SA. The Viorfa is lacking songs that they could use for their varied media, which is what MSC is for these days.  
Therefore, the WIN Trophies are just an optional game. Who knows, the losest songs gone used often in our next events in any of our Multitalent Game.

## Qualifications

- Songs must be original
  - You composed the whole music
  - You do not throw the comission to someone else, not even AI LLM.
- If Existing
  - Both Group / Artist & the Music Never been contested in this contest before. Yes, if e.g., Group is Angels of Delusion, to sing their Signature song, **yea you can**. `New Eridu` 12 points from Arenod Sediron!!! 
- If parody
  - You have received permission from author
    - e.g., if Creative Commons, anything without `NoCommercial`, & without `NoDerivatives`, means auto-approved.
- Lyrics Optional.
- You must have filling media
  - Music Video.
  - Stage Performance.
    - If No Performance, you must add Light shows
    - The composers and/or singers themselves do not need to dance. Just atleast stand among other dancers
    
## Voting

Each vote piece are **paid** with real cash, or however Dinar you got from other MultiTalent game. Just top up your Posko Gaming token. A vote costs 10 Dinar or 10 Pts.  
More votes are each own added cost.

You can only vote up to 10 each session, and you cannot vote your own country / independent city.

## Loss

If your country / independent city lost the qualification in any circumstances, you are not allowed to hinder still alive team.  
However, **you are encouraged to support whichever still alive teams** you'd like, unlimited ammount.

## Ending

All songs ever contested means that forever and ever belongs to Mosfier Record Label, and it's CC4.0-BY-SA with author whoever is involved in the making. Authors are not allowed to be litigious of the songs that been contested and forced to become royalty free, commercial use, Open source, no partial.  
But songs that even failed initial submission process won't get covered by this license rule.  
VisionDNB goal is to collect all songs for all developers to use for any media creations.
