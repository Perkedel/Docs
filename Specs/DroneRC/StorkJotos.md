# Stork Jotos mega-series

Jotos (yes, that Jotos division) drone is a combat capable enterprise series of Drone RC. This series is designed to fulfill the needs of extreme conditions in various enterprises.

Jotos drone has sub-series, where each one has been tuned according to the use case

## Series

### Duar (`D` series)

> [!NOTE]  
> alike of DJI Matrice

Duar is a tightly sealed drone designed for explosive air. It is the only few drone that complies with ATEX (`Ex` mark). Duar is designed so that is something happen inside, **it won't trigger / ignite the air around it**, assuming the unit has not been improperly repaired.

- Tightly sealed. Same multi-screw gasket enclosure method as typical gas plant systems. This exoskeleton lunchbox is the last of the last protection against the air around it, capable of containing up to 50k dynamites explosions only by about 2.5 mm thickness.
- Motor coil cemented with thermally conductive dielectric compound. Coils are tightly sealed against air and scratches (hence would trigger / ignite the air), and at the same time has chance of heat disipation. Magnetic performance is also unaffected like other open coil motors generally. This special motor also has the usual premium both bearing systems (waterproof or ceramic).
- Because it's tighly sealed, the Duar **batteries are glue-screwed inside the enclosure**. But don't worry, repair is easy (must use professional). After you unscrew the lunchbox & the batteries, there's a pair of prominent zinc lead on each side. Power these foils to activate glue burner between the contact and the battery, and you can detach it. the glue burner will also melt the connector's glue. Just make sure to clean these mess afterward and properly setup the perma-glue to properly install the new batteries. If you have any question about this, **blame Endministrator & folks at UWST!**
- Additionally, we cannot use common port to comply with Ex. Instead, ~~the data+charging port is replaced with pogo connectors, and Charge only port with wireless charging (It also has RFID data coil too)~~ the data+charging port is replaced with Wireless coil for charging & near antenna for data. You have to use included dock / base to charge and program the drone. The dock is the adapter to common plugs & ports. 
- Weight Strength priority. The drone is intentionally little bit heavier and only has large size. This design is intended to withstand strong wind.

### Capek (`C` series)

*Capek ah* is a combat Cargo series. unlike regular Cargo, Capek is Duar but Cargo design. This is useful for extreme and risky logistics. Many nation we exported it to, strictly control this model because it's considered Military use due to the design and specs.

I know it's redundant, but there could be again, package delivery in an explosive gas.

- Same as wireless only, it requires Originium to exist, work, and cooperative.

### Endfield (`E` series)

Endfield drone is a fanless combat drone. It's designed like other Endfielders combat drones generally, based on a spherical core platform that powers them.

### Terserah (`T` series) a.k.a. Suibian

This is what would be called Legacy by Endfielders. It's basically Duar Matrice, but with traditional connectors and does not use Originium Arts (hence can be released in most universe).

- The battery contacts are rubber gasketed. It still use Pogo Connector to charge & data. There's no wireless tech in here
- The drone itself has a screw open seal compartment at its port side, closing the USB-C ports. Can be used for quick debugging & data transfer if you don't have the dock with you.
- Still has coil cemented motors, because that module can be easily replaced when broke.
- Overall when batteries attached properly, it could be considered sealed, though not as much as above Originium based models, but should most of the time keep the air unignited.
