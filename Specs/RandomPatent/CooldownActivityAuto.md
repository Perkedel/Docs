# Cooldown activity auto.

Your IoT fitness system measures how tired you are after exploring malls and the tenants in them. And the car that connected to your IoT system in a whole, pre-cool the cabin as cold as how tired you are.

So when you arrived back to your car, you start the car cool like locker room. yey!

This is a AC cooling consideration addition after the typical sun stroke protection. It makes it work alongside and even beyond the sun stroke measurement, such as basement parking!
