# Slave PSU

Out of power lanes in a PSU? And/or is underpowered?

You can add a second PSU. Connect the sync cable from the master to this new slave using a smart cable (in USB-C 2 C form). These new PSU has new microcontroller that syncronize between the PSUs. Only 1 PSU need to have power button, and will automatically assignes itself as Master, and rest becomes slave.

You can now have more RAM slots, Peripherals slots, CPU slots, and more. You can even daisy chain PSUs as you like. 

Or, you can make a server rack that is multicomputer configuration, where just turn on 1 rack unit, rest units beneath turn on.

Lazy to take off power button for the slave? the system knows that! Just press power on one of the unit, all connected units will power on at the same time.  
Also, if a unit shutdown, you can configure whether or not to send shutdown to rest of the units, just some, or leave them as is.
