# Rusher

A cheating device used to rush the lores out when the circumstances is way too complicated to resolve, lazy, or times running out.

E.g., you have just acquired a company who holds this lore. And you found at the end of the story, the subject was found in an unacceptable state. Either for when you attempted, or just too lazy, you will use this Rusher to make the subject somehow returned from such bad state.

This obviously will ruin the fun, but is too crucial not to use.  
Rushers while rarely used, since Joel's elderly age has began its increasing frequency of use. Many factors such as conflicting agreements to how should it goes, etc. Ultimately Rushers had to be used on many of those lores there.

Rushers can also be used to aleviate egregious rules imposed before the acquirement or before fanfic. Note, that Rushers won't help you prevent backlash and litigations. Such shield against them, has the theoretical idea called Litiblocker, but alas the tool to this day has yet to exist due to complicated humanity & political complex.
