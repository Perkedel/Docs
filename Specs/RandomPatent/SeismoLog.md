# SEISMO LOG

Implement geo motion detection on a machine.

Seismo Log is also a standalone seisogram that can broadcast quake motions to your IoT devices and the server.

Seismo Log also has different methods such as Infrasonic impact detection, useful to detect quake shockwave earlier than before, ala animals. If your cat suddenly panik over a huge thud you can't hear, this Seismo Log also hears it at the same time.
