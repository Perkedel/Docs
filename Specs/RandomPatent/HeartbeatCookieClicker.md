# Heartbeat Cookie Clicker??

Each heartbeat is a click. You always collect clicks automatically as your heart beats. Click is currency

You can buy with Clicks

- Treadmill
- VR game
- Adrenaline injector
- Pacemaker

Each these can be interacted, some are accessories that activate permanently. Activate to make heartbeat faster. You have energy limit.

Restore energy using bed, included for free. Once clock reaches 21, you'll automatically sleep and restoring energy.

There are other upgrades to that each beat of the heart no longer just 1x click, but multiplies per upgrades you have.

Rebirth is not exist. Instead, you create a new woman. This woman is brand new, and each new woman created adds multiplier to all of them. You can access previous women too, unlike other games with rebirth. More women, better click farm!  
Base is say, 100k clicks. Increases by 1.5x per buy.
Sometimes you don't get woman, you get man! more mix also adds spices! But all save starts woman first.

You can also participate your own heartbeat into the game! Each beat also contributes to the click with multiplier upgrades you bought. Just connect heart rate monitor and bind the game to it. You can fitness to get more rewards!  
Max 210 BPM to be considered! Excession will be ignored and caps! If your heart reaches over 150 BPM for more than 120 seconds will be recapped back to 100 BPM! Do not abuse your heart (Smoking, vaping, needling, electrocution, etc.)
