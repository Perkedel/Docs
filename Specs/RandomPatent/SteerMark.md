# Steer Mark

an Orange Steering wheel icon. all cars sold and registered in DNB require to have this badge pasted on where the steering wheel is, which all of them, by the starboard. Must be clear, large and reflective.

This is because people can visit outside DNB owned realm and division. So it's marked to tell where the driver is.

also, the required reflective backlight must be there to help where the badge is.
