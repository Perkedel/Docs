# Steth Coin BLE

Wireless Bluetooth vlogging mic but it's a stethoscope

We took the core of BLE lavalier mic, compress it into pancake and repack it as such.

Once again, Recording 🫀 heart organ becomes agnostic and no longer ties to dedicated app. Any recording software works, and you can add video in your OBS live yey! You can even add audio based ECG too!

The kit is colored white as in medical white, all hospital themed, would be Enterprise use asset at hospitals.
