# Throw Pie See Future

Prank pie that's used to tell target to see future if they do this.

Set the pie mode either `Past` or `Future`. Then throw it to the target's face. The Pie then will activate and inject oracles of his/her future based on current will of statement. Once done, the Pie deactivates and its bowl melts off the face.

The target should contemplate.
