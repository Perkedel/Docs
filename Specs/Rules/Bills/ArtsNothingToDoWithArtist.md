# Arts Nothing to do with Artist

Initiator: Joel Robert Justiawan  
Short Description: Make creative works removable from its original author to be reattached to a new subject without requiring complex instructions

## Contributors

- Joel Robert Justiawan

## Wishes

This Bill Proposal wishes for the following rules to be applied:

- Clause for if `Artist Proven Guilty`
  - Turn all works both existing & subsequent into Public Domain, waiving all the ownership & copyrights
  - This includes above Initiator & Contributors, no self-imunity.

## Descriptions & Notes

The Arts Nothing To Do With Artist bill was conceived circa early 2020s. That time, there were numerous case of great authors who either found to be bad at a later event, did grave crimes, were doing bad but only did this great once or some, etc. As the result, having to use such arts made by those authors often times cringe the users that were about using. This bill will create a culture where all creative works existing and subsequent be discreditable without further modification in prior.

From the about of a work that says

> by XYZ  
> Company Name
> CC4.0-BY-SA

Can be edited next print into

> by ABC  
> New Company  
> CC0

All without having to remake out of the work itself in prior.

In addition, This action shall only be allowed to work if the author and the main contributors found to be guilty of certain crimes that is worthy of their whole removal.  
The subsequent rendition made based on the work can still be licensed to whatever accordingly.

Formerly, DNB has a rule that strictly enforces acknowledgement to the authors and their contributors of the works be used either as a dependency or a rendition to the works themselves. i.e. `Pls credit and sauce fully`. This bill will outright modify this rule to add another clause, so if an artist or companies of artists are proven guilty, the entire works both existing and subsequent can be confiscated back into the public, turning them Public Domain for the rest of the defined sentence time up to permanent.

Difference being that the original author of the existing works won't be discredited, and only the subsequent work cannot use the suspect author. e.g.
